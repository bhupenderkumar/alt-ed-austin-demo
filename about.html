At Alt Ed Austin, we're not just educating children - we're empowering them to become curious, compassionate, and confident leaders. As the Head of School, it's my privilege to watch our students grow and thrive in our inclusive and supportive community.

Our Mission & History

With a rich history dating back to 2005, Alt Ed Austin has been serving the educational needs of Austin families for over a decade. Our school was founded on the principles of creativity, community, and academic excellence.

Our teaching approach is centered around project-based learning, where students are encouraged to explore, create, and innovate. Our faculty is dedicated to creating a love of learning in every student, regardless of their learning style or background.

From robotics clubs to art exhibits, our extracurricular programs are designed to foster a sense of community and creativity in our students. Whether they're on the sports field, stage, or in the classroom, every Alt Ed Austin student is encouraged to shine.
