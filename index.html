Alt Ed Austin — The Difference

Discover why families across Austin trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our teachers are passionate educators with expertise in their subjects, dedicated to creating a supportive, inclusive learning environment that fosters academic excellence and character growth.

📚

Small Classes

With small class sizes, our students receive individualized attention, tailored instruction, and opportunities for meaningful feedback that help them thrive in every subject.

🏠

Safe Campus

Our secure, well-maintained campus provides a safe and healthy environment for students to learn, grow, and explore their interests.

📊

Curriculum

Our rigorous, project-based curriculum is designed to inspire curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking, while developing essential skills in reading, writing, mathematics, and more.

🌟

Whole-Child Education

At Alt Ed Austin, we believe in educating the whole child – body, mind, and spirit – through a comprehensive program that includes arts, music, physical education, and character development.

🌎

Community

Our vibrant community is built on a foundation of mutual respect, empathy, and kindness, providing students with a lifelong network of friends, mentors, and role models.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Early Childhood program is a nurturing environment where Pre-K students explore, create, and learn through play-based activities and projects.
